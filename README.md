# Customer & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze customer sales performance, revenue trends, profit contribution, and regional sales distribution.

The goal of this dashboard is to help understand overall business performance, identify high-performing product categories, track revenue growth, and highlight key customers and regions contributing to sales.

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Tableau Public
- SQL
- CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization
- Dashboard Design

## Dashboard Features

- Revenue Trend Analysis
- Revenue by Country Map
- Revenue by Category
- Profit Contribution Analysis
- Top Customers by Revenue
- KPI Cards:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Profit
  - Total Orders

## Key Business Insights

- Revenue showed a steady upward trend over time, indicating consistent sales growth.
- The Bikes category generated the highest contribution to both revenue and profit, making it the strongest-performing category.
- Certain countries contributed significantly higher sales compared to others, showing clear regional revenue concentration.
- Top customers accounted for a major portion of overall revenue, highlighting the importance of high-value customers.
- Profit and revenue patterns help identify which products and regions should be prioritized for business growth.
